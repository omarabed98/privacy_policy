**Last Updated:** (add date)

Thank you for using the **Irbid Basket App** (“the App”).

For any questions, contact us at: **support@irbidbasket.com**

## 1. Introduction

Irbid Basket is an e-commerce application that allows users to browse products, place orders, and receive fast delivery.  
We are committed to protecting your privacy and keeping your data secure.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
We may collect:
- Full name  
- Phone number  
- Email address  
- Delivery address  
- Order history  
- Payment method details (handled securely by third-party providers)

### 2.2 Device Information
The App may automatically collect:
- Device model and OS  
- IP address  
- App version  
- Device identifiers  

### 2.3 Location Information
We collect your location (if enabled) to:
- Provide accurate delivery  
- Suggest nearby services  
- Improve delivery accuracy

You may disable location access, but some features may not work properly.

### 2.4 Usage Information
We collect:
- App activity  
- Search and browsing behavior  
- Interaction logs  
- Crash reports and diagnostics  

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:
- Process and deliver your orders  
- Manage your account  
- Improve app performance and user experience  
- Provide customer support  
- Send notifications and updates  
- Prevent fraud and ensure security  

---

## 4. Sharing Your Information

We **do not sell** your personal information.

We may share data only with:
- **Delivery drivers** to complete your order (name, address, phone number)  
- **Service providers** for payment processing, analytics, notifications, and hosting  
- **Legal authorities** when required by law  

All third-party partners follow strict privacy and security standards.

---

## 5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data from:
- Unauthorized access  
- Misuse  
- Modification  
- Disclosure  

While we strive for full protection, no system is 100% secure.

---

## 6. Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as necessary for:
- Order fulfillment  
- Legal requirements  
- Account operation  

Once no longer needed, information is deleted or anonymized.
