## Common Issues

### 1. Order Not Delivered
- Check your order status in the app.
- Ensure your address and phone number are correct.
- If the problem continues, contact support.

### 2. Payment Issues
- Make sure your payment method is valid.
- Try again after checking your internet connection.

### 3. App Not Working Properly
- Close and reopen the app.
- Check for the latest available update.
- Restart your device if needed.
