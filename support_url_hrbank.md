## HR Bank Support

Need help with HR Bank? We are here to assist you.

### Contact Support
- Email: support@hrbank360.com  
- Response time: within 1–2 business days

### Include in your request
- App name: HR Bank  
- App version: 1.0.1+2  
- Device model and OS version  
- Short description of the issue  
- Screenshots (if available)

### Account & Access
For login, role permissions, and employee data issues, please contact your HR administrator first.

### Technical Issues
If the app crashes or does not load correctly:
1. Close and reopen the app  
2. Check internet connection  
3. Update to the latest version  
4. Contact support if the issue continues
