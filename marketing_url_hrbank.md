## HR Bank

HR Bank is a modern employee self-service app that simplifies daily HR tasks in one secure experience.

### What you can do with HR Bank
- Track attendance and daily activity
- Submit leave and vacation requests
- Create overtime and loan requests
- Review and act on approvals (for authorized users)
- Access employee profile and work information

### Why HR Bank
- Faster HR processes for employees and managers
- Clear request statuses and approval visibility
- Secure login and organization-based access
- Clean, easy-to-use mobile interface
- Arabic and English language support

### Built for teams
HR Bank helps organizations reduce manual HR work and improve employee experience through digital workflows.

### Get started
Ask your HR administrator for your company domain and login credentials, then sign in and start managing your HR requests from anywhere.
