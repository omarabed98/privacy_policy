---

## Common Issues

### 1. I cannot log in
- Make sure you are using the correct phone number or credentials provided by Irbid Basket.
- Check that you have a stable internet connection.
- If the problem continues, contact us at **support@irbidbasket.com**.

### 2. I am not receiving new orders
- Confirm that you are logged in and available/online in the app.
- Make sure location services (GPS) and notification permissions are enabled.
- Restart the app and try again.

### 3. Location is not accurate
- Enable **High Accuracy** mode in your device location settings.
- Ensure GPS is turned on and the app has permission to access your location.
