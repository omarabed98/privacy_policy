---

## 1. Who this applies to

This policy applies to users of RepFlow X (for example, sales representatives and authorized staff) and, where relevant, the organization that provides you access (“your organization”).

## 2. Information we collect

Depending on how your organization configures the App and your device permissions, we may collect:

### 2.1 Information you provide

- Account and profile details (for example, name, username, employee ID, phone number, email).
- Business transaction data you enter or sync through the App (for example, orders, invoices, payments, collections, customers, inventory movements, notes).

### 2.2 Information collected automatically

- Device and diagnostic information (for example, device model, operating system version, app version, crash logs).
- Security and fraud-prevention signals (for example, authentication events, IP address or network identifiers when connecting to servers).
- Approximate location **only if** your organization enables location-related features and you grant permission.

### 2.3 Information from third parties

We may receive information from your organization’s systems (for example, ERP/accounting backends) when those systems are integrated with the App.

- Sync and display sales and operational data as authorized by your organization.
- Improve reliability and performance, troubleshoot issues, and maintain security.
- Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.
