## Marketing URL (what it is)

On **App Store Connect** (iOS/macOS), **Marketing URL** is an **optional public link** to your product’s **marketing site** — the page that sells or explains the app/brand (features, screenshots, contact, “Request a demo,” etc.). It is **not** required for approval by itself; users may open it from the store listing.

**Google Play** does not use that exact label; the closest thing is your **developer website** or any link you put in the listing description — same idea: a **promotional / corporate** page, not internal APIs or admin consoles.

---

## What you should put there

Use a normal **HTTPS** page that:

- Names the product (**Cash Van** / your official product name).
- Briefly describes **who it’s for** (e.g. field sales, van teams, distributors).
- Highlights **main benefits** (orders, collections, reports, printing — whatever you actually ship).
- Has **clear calls to action**: contact sales, request demo, or link to download from the store.

**Avoid:** staging URLs, raw IPs, login-only portals, API base URLs, or blank “under construction” pages — stores and reviewers prefer something professional and stable.
